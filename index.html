FAQ

What is Voice-Y?
Voice-Y is a new way to express yourself online. I love reading articles and blogs online but I get frustrated when all I can do to contribute is post a comment that nobody can see or share it on my Facebook wall. I wanted to write articles to support, counter, and contribute to other writer's articles but I can't do that with today's news sites... I suddenly realized that I had to make the site that allows it's users to do that.
Who is Voice-Y for?
As the name suggests, Voice-Y is primarily for people who fall into Generation Y (sometimes referred to as "the Millennials"). That being said, this site is not restrictive. Anyone with a Facebook account can log in and write an article or participate in a discussion. This site is all about people voicing their opinion and providing information for each other; the more people that contribute, the more effective the site will be.
Why should I write for Voice-Y?
Great question! There are many reasons why writing is a great thing for everyone to do. I'll highlight the main points:
  • Your voice will be seen by tons of your peers. Validate your opinions, learn more about yourself, the views of others, and see just how many people share your opinion on a subject.
  • Use this site as a resume builder. Now more than ever companies want people with experience, even for entry level jobs. This contradiction is a huge road block for anyone trying to start a career in any field. Write about what you love, write about what you know, write about something that will be received well by your peers and use it as a portfolio piece to present to the company you wish to work for.
  • Eventually there will be monetary rewards. I plan on implementing a system that gives writers a percentage of the ad revenue they bring in with their articles. This means the people who write the most popular articles will see a tangible return on their time investment!
